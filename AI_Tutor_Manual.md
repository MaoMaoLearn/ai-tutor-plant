# AI Tutor: Quick Start Manual

Welcome to your personal AI Tutor! This 1-page guide will help you get started with exploring new concepts and ideas using the app.

---

## 1. Setting Up Your Session
When you first open the app, you'll be greeted by the **Setup Screen**.
- **Your Name:** Enter your name so the tutor can personalize your experience.
- **Topic of Interest:** Enter any topic you want to learn about today (e.g., "Photosynthesis", "Black Holes", "The Industrial Revolution").
- Click **Start Exploring** to begin your learning adventure!

## 2. Chatting with Your Tutor
Once your session starts, you'll enter the **Chat Interface**.
- **Interactive Conversation:** The AI Tutor will welcome you and ask an introductory question about your topic to gauge your current understanding.
- **Sending Messages:** Use the input box at the bottom of the screen to answer questions, ask your own questions, or share your thoughts. Press **Enter** or click the **Send icon** to reply.
- **AI Reflections (Metacognition):** You may notice small text blocks above or inside the AI's main messages. These are the AI's "thought processes" where it explains *why* it is asking you a specific question or guiding you in a certain direction.

## 3. Ending the Session & Getting Your Cheat Sheet
When you feel you've learned enough about the topic or want to wrap up:
- Click the **End Session** button located in the top right corner of the chat header.
- The AI will process your entire conversation and generate a personalized **Cheat Sheet** based on exactly what you discussed.
- This summary acts as a perfect study resource for the concepts you just learned.

## 4. Starting a New Topic
Done with your current topic and ready for more?
- At the bottom of your Cheat Sheet, simply click the **Restart** button to return to the Setup Screen.
- Enter a new topic and begin a fresh learning session!

---

### 💡 Pro Tips for the Best Experience
- **Don't be afraid to say "I don't know":** The tutor is designed to guide you. If you're stuck, just say so, and the tutor will help break down the problem.
- **Ask follow-up questions:** If you don't fully understand an explanation, ask the tutor to simplify it, provide an analogy, or give a real-world example.
- **Think out loud:** The more detail you provide in your answers, the better the AI can tailor its guidance to your specific learning style.
